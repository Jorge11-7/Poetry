## How to update

**Option A — Tell me and I'll do it:**
Just reply to the pull request (or the chat) with your email and Instagram handle, and I will update all the files for you automatically.

**Option B — Edit it yourself in GitHub:**
1. Open [`portfolio/index.html`](./portfolio/index.html) in GitHub
2. Click the ✏️ pencil icon (top right of the file view)
3. Find the three lines that say `[tu correo aquí / your email here]`, `[tu sitio web / your website]`, and `[@tu_usuario]`
4. Replace each one with your real info
5. Scroll to the bottom and click **Commit changes**
